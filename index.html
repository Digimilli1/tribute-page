Mark Zuckerberg

The Man Who Created America's Social Network

This is Mark Zuckerberg's Timeline to one of the most powerful magnates.

  • May 1984 - Born in White Plains, NY
  • October 1995 - Learns computer programming for the first time
  • July 1999 - Starts his first company
  • September 2002 - Gets accepted and starts at Harvard
  • February 2004 - Creates Facebook in his dorm room with friends
  • December 2004 - Drops out of Harvard and moves to California
  • May 2007 - Named the world's youngest billionaire
  • July 2010 - Facebook reaches 500 million users
  • September 2010 - Mark donates $100 million dollars to Newark, NJ Public Schools
  • October 2010 - Mark's life is turned into movie "The Social Network"
